Balogna Sandwich Recipe

Description

Poor man's steak. This is what my parents called balogna when I was growing up. It's cheap, it's tasty, and it's easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Balogna
  • 2 slices of bread
  • cheese
Steps
  1. Put slice bread on plate
  2. put balogna on bread
  3. put cheese on top of balogna
  4. put second slice of bread on top of cheese
  5. Enjoy your sandwich!
